What is CoderDojo?

CoderDojo is a free and open non-profit group founded to teach youth (ages 7-17) to code. Our sessions are located at the McDonough Public Library and are open to all Henry County youth and parents/guardians.

Register for a session

At a CoderDojo event the only limit on what you can do is your imagination. You can learn new programming languages, create apps for your computer, tablet or phone, build websites, make games and much, much more. There is only one rule: “Above all; Be cool”. View and sign up for our upcoming Dojos.
